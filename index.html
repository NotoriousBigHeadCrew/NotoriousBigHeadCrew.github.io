Welcome to NHBC


Notorious Big Head Crew (NBHC) , is a deflationary collection of 7777 NFTs ruling the blocks on the Solana network. Each crew member is completely unique, hand drawn and assembled by code. Unfortunately, not all will survive the notorious lifestyle, many will be killed and burnt during the takeover. For more, refer our Roadmap




Lore

A devastating time to be alive in Nimano, a country that permits no individual freedom and seeks authority in all aspects of an individual life. The government holds tight control over the nation, creating an unpleasant and unlivable environment for the people. Something must be done to save the people and set them free. 7777 Notorious Big Heads have formed the strongest crew with a mission to take over Nimano and bring freedom, innovation and create a positive social impact for the people. The Takeover has begun and we must stick together

Roadmap

Stage One


Main Launch

7777 NBHC members will be released onto the Solana Network with a mission to take over Nimano and set the people free.


Stage Two


“The Blueprint”

NBHC holders will be Verified on Discord and will be able to access the “The Blueprint” Channel. The team will partner up with Grape Protocol to enable a community DAO to vote for future decisions, collaborations with other projects and much more future utility.


Stage Three

The Takeover (Season One) Pre Release

“The Takeover'' will be season one of our animated show series illustrating the devastating times living in Nimano and the destruction of the government caused by the NBHC. To accomplish a successful takeover we must brainstorm a strategic plan together. DAO members will have voting rights on the script and behind the season access on the series. During the creation of the series we will be sweeping the floor on all secondary markets. Crew members that were swept are the exact ones that will be killed in our series, burnt and removed from the circulation.


Stage Four

Collaboration

Collaborations of other NFT projects will be decided by the DAO members. We will be sweeping the floor of the community chosen project. Once swept they will be included into season one of our film series. Some will survive (those will be airdropped to members) and some will be killed (those will be kept in a wallet NEVER touched).


Stage Five

The Takeover (Season One) Release

DAO members will have exclusive access to see every episode before the public release.
